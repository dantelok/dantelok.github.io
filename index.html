About me

I am an AI Engineer and Researcher at LifeHikes. My main area of interest is Natural Language Processing and Speech Processing, especially in Natural Language Understanding. But I also have some works in Computer Visions.

During my undergraduate studies, I took many math and finance related courses at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University since I was in love with math and hoped I can apply them to make a fortune. After graduated, I joined few banks and financial institution as a finance position, but I found that meaningless thinking all about money every day and night. Before starting my Machine Learning research career, I spent some time in Citrix as a Data Analyst, to take new challenges and look for a way to make a bigger impact. Then, I took the machine learning course by Andrew Ng and started applying the knowledge to my work in Citrix. I built the first machine learning project predicting the sales and potential customers in our Hong Kong Marketing team at Citrix. At that time, I found the AI applications is fascinating and I want to be a part of the community. Finally, I landed my first machine learning job. In my first machine learning job as an Analyst Programmer at IconTek, I built the whole testing demo and integrated a trained chatbot into it. Unfortunately, I experienced a layoff during the recession in 2019.

In 2020, I joined LifeHikes as their first AI Engineer.
